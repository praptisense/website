Maya Mirror

A virtual Dressing room

Mayamirror enables your customers to try on clothes to check the fit and style virtually, rather than physically.

Your entire clothing line is created virtually and maintained within our application.

Your customers select garments based on their requirements and budget and then try them on virtually.

This process is very easy and fun and allows your customers to try on an unlimited amount of clothing.

Mayamirror virtual garments are photo realistic and the application allows your customers to capture their image, with the virtual clothing on, to share with their friends and family instantly.
